ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Joseph Watson. I am a computer science student at UNC Charlotte with a passion for building software that not only works well behind the scenes but also looks polished on the surface. I enjoy the problem-solving side of backend logic, but I also like bringing things to life visually and making sure the end result feels smooth, presentable, and intuitive.

Outside of computer science, I enjoy playing soccer, working out, and allowing myself to grow as a professional developer. This website is where I share my work, ideas, and overall professional growth.
